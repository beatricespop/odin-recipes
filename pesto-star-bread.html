Pesto Star Bread with Thermomix

Description

Delicious bread with a twist

Ingredients

  1. Dough
    • 115g milk
    • 60g water
    • 15g dried instant yeast
    • 1 large egg
    • 35g olive oil
    • 10g garlic powder
    • 5g fine sea salt
  2. Pesto
    • 250g mozzzarella
    • 40g pecorino cheese
    • 10g pine nuts
    • 2 garlic cloves
    • 40g fresh basil leaves
    • 70g extra virgin oil
    • 10g fine sea salt
  3. Assembly
    • 1 medium egg
    • 120g passata
    • 10g garlic power
    • 10g dried oregano
    • 5g ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Place milk, water and yeast in mixing bowl, then warm 2 min/37°C/speed 1.
  2. Add flour, oil, egg, garlic powder and salt, then knead Dough 5 min. Transfer to greased bowl, cover, and leave to rise until doubled in size (approx. 1 hour).
  3. Grate mozzarella in mixing bowl 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  4. Grate pecorino in mixing bowl 15 sec/speed 10. Set aside.
  5. Add pine nuts, basil, oil, garlic and salt, then chop 20 sec/speed 7. Transfer pesto to a bowl and set aside.
  6. On a lightly floured surface, divide rested dough into four equal-sized pieces and shape into balls. Roll out one ball into a disc (Ø 28 cm). Place on baking tray and brush border with egg wash.
  7. Spread passata over dough, leaving a 2 cm border. Sprinkle with garlic powder, oregano, and pepper.
  8. Roll out second ball of dough (Ø 25 cm), place on top, and brush border with egg wash. Spread pesto, leaving 2 cm border.
  9. Roll out third ball of dough (Ø 25 cm), place on top, brush with egg wash. Sprinkle grated mozzarella, leaving a 2 cm border. Roll last ball of dough into a disc (Ø 25 cm) and place on top.
  10. Press a 5 cm cookie cutter gently in the center (without cutting through). Slice dough into 16 equal strips from center to edge.
  11. Take two adjacent strips, twist them away from each other twice, then pinch ends together to form star-like points. Repeat with all strips.
  12. Cover with greased cling film and leave to prove in a warm place for approx. 45 min. Preheat oven to 200°C near the end of proving.
  13. Brush with egg wash. Bake 15–17 min at 200°C until golden brown.
  14. Cool on tray for 5 minutes, then serve warm so guests can tear off pieces.
