Marry Me Chickpeas and Orzo

Description

A perfect 20-minute dinner! Chickpeas and orzo in a luscious, creamy, max-flavor tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbs olive oil
  • 1 shallot, thinly sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 2 tbs tomato paste
  • 1 cup orzo
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • a small bundle of fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the shallot and the garlic; sauté for 2-3 minutes until soft and fragrant. Add the chickpeas and tomato paste; sauté for another 2-3 minutes until it has developed a deeper red color.
  2. Cook Orzo: Add the orzo, chicken broth, and thyme sprigs; bring to a low simmer. Cover and cook for 7-9 minutes.
  3. Finish Sauce: Remove the thyme sprigs. Keep the heat on low. Stir in the cream and simmer for 2-3 more minutes, until it’s totally luscious and creamy. Taste and adjust seasonings, adding more cream if you want.
  4. Add-Ins: Add your sun dried or fresh tomatoes and spinach, and stir them in until the spinach is wilted. Serve sprinkled with fresh herbs (I like thyme) and Parmesan.
